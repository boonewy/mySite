Sleeping

I have been known to sleep up to 10 hours a day. My personal best is a whopping 26 hours! Only through through many years of rigorous napping and an utter disregard for the circadian rhythm have I been able to achieve this. I could thank the unwavering support of my friends and family, but honestly all credit goes to myself and my complete lack of self discipline.

Trail Cycling

I picked up trail cycling in poly as a way to relax and relieve stress. The technical challenge of riging on the trail requires one's full attention - forcing me to empty my mind of the day's worries and focus on the matter at hand. As a restless 17 year old, it also served as an avenue for physical (cardiovascular) release. I could burn all that accumulated energy from sitting in classes and lectures all day.
